About Me

Full-Stack Developer in Austin, TX

B.Totsch

Originally from North Carolina, I made my way to Texas after earning my Bachelor of Science in Clinical Laboratory Science at UNC Chapel Hill. From there I gained valuable work experience in a hospital environment. During this time, I met my husband, Mark, and together we tackled the renovation of a 1955 house that became our first home together.

As exciting as my home life was, I started to develop an itch for more challenging work. I felt like I was no longer engaging my curiosity and desire to learn. One of my personal mantras in life is that I should never complain about something that I can change. So, with a nudge from my friends in the tech industry and my husband's generous support, I left the lab to pursue a new career in Web Development. I spent 3 months learning at Galvanize in Austin, TX as a web development immersive student. I built a solid foundation in JavaScript, CSS/HTML while exploring a full-stack curriculum. Since then, I've ventured into the React.js framework and continue to expand my tech stack daily. I'm looking forward to growing my skill set with work experience and further research into emerging technologies.
